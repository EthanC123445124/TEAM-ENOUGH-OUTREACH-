Why students and parents should get involved

Clear safety information can help families act before there is a crisis.

Schools already send annual parent notifications. Students, parents, and community members can ask districts to make firearm safety and safe storage reminders easier to find, easier to understand, and repeated throughout the year.

About the creator

Ethan Cheung

Ethan Cheung created this project after working with Brady United and becoming involved with Team ENOUGH, Brady's youth program. His summer research focused on how California public unified school districts communicate gun safety and safe firearm storage information to parents through annual parental notifications.

This resource is built from that research so students, parents, and community members can find the right district contact, review what families currently receive, and start a practical conversation about making safety information easier to see.
